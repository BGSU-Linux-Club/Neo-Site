What is the BG Linux Club?

The BG Linux Club is a student organization dedicated to the use of open technology in the Bowling Green community. We strive to serve the public good by hosting educational events, developing open content, assisting other organizations, and engaging in outreach.

Located in windy Bowling Green, Ohio!
