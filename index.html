What is the BG Linux Club?

The BG Linux Club is a student organization in Bowling Green State University that serves as the centerpiece of hacker culture and software ethics. We meet regularly to share our interests in operating systems and software freedom. There's also tutorials for students who wish to learn more about the GNU/Linux operating system and related software tools.

Who are you?

I'm Ian Ressa, the president of Linux Club. It's my fourth year, and I major in Mathematics and Computer Science. You can find my personal website here. I started Linux Club because it didn't exist yet, and I wanted to share my interest for Free Software and computing in general.
